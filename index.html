About Our Institution

St John’s Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Gudiyattam is run by the Society of the Sisters of St. Anne, Chennai. Started in 1969, it functions with the Vision and Mission of the Congregation and imparts Holistic Education to create a New and Just Society. Imparting quality education and relevance to the marginalized sections of society.
